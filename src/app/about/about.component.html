BIO
My name might sound a bit strange, but I assure you, it is the name my parents gave me. I am from Dhaka, the bustling heart of Bangladesh. Despite its hectic pace, there is a unique charm to the city that inevitably captivates its inhabitants. Dhaka is ever-thriving, a place where something exciting is always on the horizon.
Yet, my heart holds a special place for Chittagong, my hometown—a wonderful city nestled amid hills and the sea.

I completed my Bachelor of Science in Electrical and Electronic Engineering from Ahsanullah University of Science and Technology in June 2022. My time at university has been the best part of my life so far. From the nerve-wracking lab exams to mastering the complexities of Engineering math, these experiences were intertwined with building an amazing circle of friends and exploring the wonders of Bangladesh during semester breaks.

Upon completing my Bachelor's in Electrical and Electronic Engineering, I started my journey as a programmer. The excitement of my first job still resonates with me. Alongside this professional path, my passion for painting, nurtured since a young age, continued to flourish. Each stroke of my brush reminds me different moments and emotions—a reflection of my feelings, struggles, and triumphs.

Furthermore, I dedicated a decade to theater acting, starting from my childhood. These varied experiences have woven together to shape the intricate tapestry of my life, each representing a fragment of my mind in different situations.
