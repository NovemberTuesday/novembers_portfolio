Through art, I find healing, inspiration, and a timeless connection to my innermost self. Each painting of mine holds a unique emotion, reflecting the experiences I had in life.
Some pieces remind me of a tough time that I passed through and come this far, while others bring back cherished memories spent with loved ones.
And then there are those spontaneous creations, when I was just having a weekend and found something creative to pass my time on.

Theater Artist
I entered into the world of theater as a child artist. For all these years, alongside my academic pursuits I always managed time for Theater.
While acting remained my primary focus, I actively participated in various aspects of theater production . This included assisting costume designers, managing props, setting up stages, and even overseeing event management.
I've had the opportunity to act in dramas staged in various locations across Bangladesh and India.
